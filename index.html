VR Shapes

On Quest: open in the browser → Enter VR.

Left stick — move (relative to gaze). Right stick — smooth turn.

A — jump. B — sprint (hold to move fast).

Trigger / hand pinch — grab a shape. Throw it and it flies.

Left X — spawn a shape. Left Y — black hole. Left grip — force blast.

Shapes fall, bounce and collide (Earth-like gravity).

Put the controllers down — hand tracking takes over.
